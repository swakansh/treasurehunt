RULES

  • There are three questions as of now. Each question has a max score of 100.
  • There are two types of hint available for each question: Normal hints and Bonus hints.

      Normal Hints

    • There are four normal hints. Each comes at an interval of 1 minute from the start of the question.
    • There is no mark deduction for using normal hints.

      • Bonus Hints

    • There are two bonus hints available.
    • First bonus hint gets unlocked 6th minute after the start of the question and second 8th minute after the start of the question.
    • Using First bonus hints costs you 19 points and second 29 points.
  • There is an overall deduction in your score depending upon how much time you spent on a question.
    • If you take more than 1 minute, you lose 5 points.
    • If you take more than 2 minutes, you lose 7 points.
    • If you take more than 3 minutes, you lose 11 points.
  • If two or more players have same score, they will be ranked according to their last successfull attempt.
